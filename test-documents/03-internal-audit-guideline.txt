Internal Audit Guideline: Regulatory Closure Evidence
Section 1: Audit evidence for all high-risk regulatory action points must be retained for eight years.
Section 2: Compliance teams must upload closure notes, screenshots, SOP references, and approval records.
Section 3: Audit teams must independently verify whether submitted evidence satisfies the original regulatory obligation.
Section 4: Failed evidence submissions must be returned to the owning department within 3 business days.
Section 5: Enterprise risk committees must review overdue high-risk compliance gaps every month.
