SEBI/HO/2026/Investor Protection Notification
Section 1: Banks providing investment distribution services must update customer disclosure templates within 15 days.
Section 2: Mis-selling complaints must be escalated to the compliance officer within 24 hours.
Section 3: Legal teams must review distributor agreements and update investor protection clauses.
Section 4: Operations teams must publish revised customer communication scripts for branch and call center staff.
Section 5: Audit teams must verify evidence of disclosure implementation during the next internal audit cycle.
